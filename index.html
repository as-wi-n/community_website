About Us

Who We Are

TinkerHub NSS College of Engineering Palakkad is a passionate community of student innovators, creators, and tech enthusiasts. We aim to foster a culture of learning, collaboration, and building cool things together.

Whether you're a beginner coder, a design pro, or just curious about technology, there's a place for you here. We organize workshops, hackathons, speaker sessions, and more.

Inviting industry experts and experienced alumni to share their knowledge and insights.
